Privacy Policy

At Bhvesa Classes, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website, enquiry forms, calls, messages, or other communication channels.

This Privacy Policy explains how Bhvesa Classes collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By visiting our website or submitting any enquiry form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We May Collect

Information You Provide to Us

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you fill out an enquiry form, contact us by phone, email, WhatsApp, or visit our institute.

  • Student name
  • Parent or guardian name, where applicable
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Class, course, or subject interest
  • Message, query, or admission-related details

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, some basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and general website usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information collected from you for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or admission request
  • To share course details, batch timings, fees, and demo class information
  • To provide academic or digital marketing course-related support
  • To improve our website, services, and communication process
  • To contact you through phone, email, SMS, or WhatsApp regarding your enquiry
  • To maintain internal records for admission and communication purposes

3. Sharing of Information

Bhvesa Classes does not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary:

  • With staff or faculty members for admission, counselling, or class-related communication
  • With service providers who help us operate our website or communication systems
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience, understand website traffic, and enhance site performance. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function properly.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites, social media pages, or third-party platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those external websites. Please review their privacy policies before submitting any information.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request correction, update, or deletion of your personal information. You may also ask us not to contact you for promotional or informational communication.

8. Children’s Information

Since Bhvesa Classes provides academic coaching for school students, information may be submitted by parents or guardians. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise the sharing of any personal information related to minors.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
